3. A Multi-Agent Land-Use and Transportation Model Suite for Baltimore-Washington Region as a Future Mobility Test Bed

Funded by: Maryland Transportation Institute, University of Maryland

Timeline: 2019-2020

Main Responsibilities:

  • Collaborated with multidisciplinary teams to develop the model.
  • Developed a Land Use Model: Simple Integrated Land-Use Orchestrator (SILO) incorporating a population synthesizer for the State of Maryland.
  • Processed big data for adoption in the population synthesis and travel demand model inputs.
  • Developed a hybrid trip-based travel demand model: Microsimulation Transport Orchestrator (MITO) for current and future year demand forecast.
  • Developed an agent-based transportation assignment and simulation model: Multi-Agent Transport Simulation (MATSim) for transportation modes including transit to serve as a policy-sensitive tool for the state and policymakers.
